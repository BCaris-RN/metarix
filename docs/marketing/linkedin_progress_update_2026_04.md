# MetaRix LinkedIn Progress Update — April 2026

## Investor-facing LinkedIn post

We’ve reached an important milestone in the **public build of MetaRix**.

The current product walkthrough now demonstrates a connected operating flow across the full marketing lifecycle: **Strategy, Planning, Workflow, Schedule, Reports, Listening, Assets, Activity, and Admin**.

This is meaningful because most teams still manage growth, approvals, reporting, and campaign execution across fragmented tools with limited operational visibility. MetaRix is being built to unify those motions into a structured workspace where teams can move from campaign intent to execution with clearer governance, approval posture, reporting context, and operational traceability.

What the public build now shows:
- strategy and audience planning
- campaign and editorial planning
- workflow and approval-state visibility
- schedule readiness and publish posture
- reporting and performance summaries
- listening and competitor monitoring
- asset organization and usage context
- activity timelines and admin visibility

This is still a build in progress, but it is increasingly demonstrating the shape of a real operating system rather than a concept alone.

We’re focused on building a more coordinated, audit-friendly, and execution-ready environment for modern marketing operations.
